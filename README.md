#About the Moto

It is a Product Orders management app, where it keep track of Pending requested orders , Pending Seller Orders and Completed Orders

When a buyer requests Orders ,


  1  If the seller is ready with orders then he will Sell and The order is completed and it is stored in completed orders
  
  2 If the seller is not ready with , then it is stored in Pending Buyer Orders and whenever the Product is ready to Sell it automatically stored as Sold and stored in completed Orders
  
When a Seller posts Orders,


  1  If the buyer is ready to buy then he will Sell and The order is completed and it is stored in completed orders
  
  2 If the buyer is not ready to buy , then it is stored in Pending Seller Orders and whenever the Product is ready to Buy it automatically stored as Sold and stored in completed Orders
